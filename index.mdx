## Welcome to Vibedropper

Vibedropper helps you grow and own your audience with **email** and **list-gated drops**. Build lists, send campaigns, and run exclusive product drops that only your subscribers can shop.

## What you can do

| Feature | What it’s for |
|--------|----------------|
| **Lists & subscribers** | Organize your audience into lists, add tiers, and use segments to target the right people. |
| **Campaigns** | Design and send emails with a drag-and-drop editor. Choose who receives them and track opens and clicks. |
| **Forms** | Create signup forms and embed them on your site or share a link to grow your lists. |
| **Drops** | Run time-limited, list-gated offers. Only subscribers with access can shop your drop storefront. |
| **Orders** | See orders from your drops, mark them ready, and manage fulfillment. |
| **Settings** | Set your brand look, domains, team, regions, and API keys. |

## Your dashboard

After you sign in at [Vibedropper](https://vibedroppers.com), you’ll see your **Dashboard**. The sidebar takes you to **Subscribers**, **Campaigns**, **Forms**, **Drops**, **Pages**, **Orders**, and **Products** (when available). Use **Settings** to configure your organization and brand.
